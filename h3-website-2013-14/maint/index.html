Maintenance at Hostel 3

The Maintenance Council looks after the general maintenance of the hostel. We have various facilities made available for the students to use:-

  1. Lounge:- The lounge is equipped with carrom boards, T.T. tables, foosball table, big screen TV, a music system, as well as newspapers and magazines.
  2. T.V. Room:- The T.V room, with a big screen T.V and high quality speakers, is accessible to students.
  3. Washing:- Each wing is equipped with one washing machine and each block has one dryer.
  4. Cycle Pool:- We have six cycles which can be issued from the security guard for a fixed period.
  5. Comp Room:- The Computer Room of the hostel, recently renovated has 4 computers in perfectly working conditions for use by anyone in the hostel against I-card issue. We also have printer and scanner facility.
  6. Music Room:- We are the only hostel in the insti (\m/) to have sound-proof music room.
  7. Gym Room:- Our gym room has basic gym equipments. It has dumbells, weightlifting equipments and a weighing machine.
  8. Pool Room:- Pool room has Pool table. It can be accessed on issuing I-cards.
  9. Extra:- Hostel has iron, vaccum cleaner, scrabble; which can be issued on I-cards. Recently, all our toilets have been fitted with Soap Dispensars which are filled with Dettol on a regular basis.
  10. Complaints:- Complaint books are available with the security guard - Carpenter Complaint Book and Maint and Comp Problems Book.
All these facilities are for hostelites and we expect that all students use them responsibly, so that all of us can make the best use of these.
