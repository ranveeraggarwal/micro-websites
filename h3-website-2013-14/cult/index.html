Titillate. Arouse. Astonish. Excite. Lead.Think. Exercise. Enjoy. Live!
If there is any performing art that can do all of the above and do it the best, it is undoubtedly Dance. And at Hostel 3, Dance is not just that. Because at Hostel 3, Dance is synonymous with amazing ideas and thoughts, stories that can make you laugh and cry, and people you can learn from! From the energetic Hip-hop to the massy Bollywood and Tollywood, from impressive Party moves to the elegant Classical Choreography, from the sexy Couple to the classy Contemporary- at H3, we can do it all! Want to learn a move you saw Hrithik perform? A stunt you saw on “Dance India Dance?” Participate at college festivals? Want to impress the girl you’ve just met? One word for you: Dance!

Where? Your Hostel!! H3 will give you all you need! How? There shall be workshops and jamming sessions! And then the competitions!

Sophie Dance League (for the PGs and the Second Year UGs)- A chance for the participants to learn, explore and impress! And then the BIG DADDY- GYRATIONS! A competition that can single-handedly take your hostel to glory at the CULT GC! An event where the hostel’s crème de la crème steps out to perform at the grand Convocation Hall! So, if you’re thinking- “Mereko toh nahi aata! Humse naa ho paayega!” Don’t worry- if you join Dance@H3, rest assured- you’ll turn out a much better dancer than you’ve ever been! Who knows? Speaking is an art. If nourished and nurtured well, this art can be an indispensable tool in life. When you want to stand up for the beliefs you believe in, the way you convey your thoughts and arguments matters just as much as the content itself and hence, speaking skills are important.

As you probably know, there's an art involved in stand-up comedy and poetry -recitation too. The manner in which you hit the right word at the right time with the right expressions is of paramount importance.

In short, chahiye bandi ko patana ho ya interviewer ko, speakings skills toh phodu honi hi chahiye!

Designing is an expression of what you feel inside. Thoughts turned into a visually appealing "something", that's just good enough to bring out what the creator felt, qualifies to be called a design. From graphics to video, there's a wide range of options to explore. It's no rocket science, just your heart's aspiration and a willingness to express. Besides, a thorough google search and some basic editing functions is all you need sometimes ;)

Stand-up comedy, mime, monologues, street-play, nukkad naataks, situational acting, mono acts- we've got people who can do it all!

Literary Arts involves creative writing, wordgames, quizzing and a plethora of other activities including games such as Scrabble, Snatch, Potpourri etc. The Institute Literary Club (Literati) holds regular informal meets, which you can attend to learn more about the activities and also interact with people having similar tastes. There are 6 GCs (General Championships) pertaining to Literary Arts: English Creative Writing GC, Hindi Creative Writing GC, General Quiz GC, Informal Wordgames GC, Formal Wordgames GC, Wolfpack GC.

H3 has always had a vibrant culture when it comes to literary arts. Stalwarts such as Antariksh Bothale, Rishabh Jain, Tarun Gangwar, Omkar Nene, Avijit Arya, Vipul Singh, Pranav Shetty, Ashwin Paranjape, Harshwardhan Singh, Anubhav Mangal, Yash Tambawala have created a formidable legacy. Never having dropped out of the top 5, H3 has always been a force to reckon with when it comes to literary arts. Workshops will be held before every GC and informal meets shall also be arranged at the hostel level. My only request to you is to attend these meets and workshops, as they will benefit you greatly and help you identify your hidden talent. With your help and enthusiasm, this year shall be our year.
