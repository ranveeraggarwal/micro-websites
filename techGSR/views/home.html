Our Vision

TechGSR, Socio Tech non profit Student body of IIT Bombay, believes that it is the duty of every Techgeek and also every student to be socially responsible citizen. This initiative is a tryst to not just get involved ourselves, but also involve other students, all over nation and abroad to initiate projects for development of society by using their technical knowledge.

What we do

  • We travel - to understand places
  • We study - to understand situation
  • We innovate - to develop new ideas
  • We implement - to make the llifes simpler and ideas sustainable
