Efficient Smokeless Chulahclose

Asthama, lose of eyesight is common among women who cook food for hours in front of the chulah working on wood, which is cut daily and brought from forest. We propose a CFD modelled better design which is made up of the same substance – which is mud, from their own farms – and thus adding on no more cost to them, and at the same time increasing the turbulence and thus the heating efficiency. Result – Zero cost, Smokeless Chulah!!

A clean home is the secret to the best health of a person – This was something which lacked in the village, Hirewadi. The houses were not just unhygienic, but dark and not at all ventilated.
